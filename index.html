Our Story

Rooted in the heart of Silicon Valley

Since 1982, Cupertino Florist has been creating beautiful, meaningful floral arrangements for families, businesses, and celebrations throughout the South Bay. We are a family-owned shop with deep roots in this community.

Our expert designers pour their heart into every bouquet — whether it's a simple "thinking of you" arrangement or a grand sympathy tribute for a memorial service. We source the finest fresh flowers and take pride in our craft.
