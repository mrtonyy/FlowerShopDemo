Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering flowers from Cupertino Florist.

You can order online 24/7 through our website, or call us directly at (408) 252-3560 during business hours (Monday–Saturday, 9am–6pm). Browse our arrangements by occasion or product type, select your size, and add to cart. Checkout takes just a few minutes.
We deliver throughout Silicon Valley including Cupertino, San Jose, Santa Clara, Sunnyvale, Mountain View, Los Altos, Palo Alto, Saratoga, Los Gatos, Campbell, Milpitas, and more. See our full delivery areas page for the complete list of cities and zip codes.
Yes! We offer same-day delivery Monday through Saturday. Place your order by 2:00 PM (Monday–Friday) or 12:00 PM (Saturday) for same-day delivery. For peak holidays like Valentine's Day and Mother's Day, we recommend ordering 2–3 days in advance.
Please call us as soon as possible at (408) 252-3560. We'll do our best to accommodate changes or cancellations if your arrangement hasn't already been prepared. We cannot guarantee changes for same-day orders placed close to our cut-off time.
Yes. We regularly deliver to hospitals including El Camino, Good Samaritan, Kaiser Permanente, Lucile Packard Children's, O'Connor, and others throughout the South Bay. We also deliver to funeral homes and cemeteries. Please call us to confirm delivery arrangements for specific facilities.
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) for online orders. Phone orders may also be paid by credit card. We accept cash and checks for in-store purchases.
We source our flowers fresh daily, both locally and from growers worldwide. Our exotic and specialty flowers are flown in overnight to ensure peak freshness. Every arrangement is made fresh to order by our skilled florists.
Absolutely! We love creating custom arrangements for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions. Call us at (408) 252-3560 or use our contact form to discuss your vision. We offer traditional, high-style, and exotic design styles.
Our drivers will attempt to leave the flowers in a safe, shaded location or with a neighbor. We'll note the arrangement details on a door hanger. For temperature-sensitive flowers or if there's no safe place to leave them, we may contact you to reschedule. Providing a delivery phone number during checkout helps ensure successful delivery.
We stand behind the quality of every arrangement we create. If you're not completely satisfied, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery and we'll make it right — either by replacing the arrangement or providing a refund. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Our friendly team is here to help Monday through Saturday, 9am–6pm.
