About Cupertino Florist

A family-owned floral shop rooted in the Silicon Valley community for over 40 years. We know flowers — and we know how to help you express your feelings with them.

🌷

Our Story

Cupertino Florist is a family owned and operated business that has been serving the Silicon Valley with unique and creative floral designs and outstanding customer service for over 40 years.

From intimate birthday bouquets to elaborate wedding arrangements, our experienced staff can assist you in making just the right choice to express your thoughts through flowers or any of the other products we offer.

Whether the occasion is happy or sad, we know it's important to you that the appropriate gift be sent. We can help. We promise to exceed your expectations!
